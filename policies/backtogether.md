# A. PRIVACY POLICY

## The purpose of this Notice

This notice describes how we collect and use personal data about individuals, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation(GDPR).

You should read the following carefully in order to understand our procedures regarding your personal data and how we treat it.

## WHAT IS THE GDPR?
The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) aims to strengthen data protection for people within the European Union, replacing the EU privacy directive known as Directive 95/46/EC. This regulation came into force on 25 May 2018. For more information about the GDPR, please visit: [Link](https://ec.europa.eu/info/law/law-topic/data-protection_en)
